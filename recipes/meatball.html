Meatball Sauce Recipes

Description

Ingredients

  • Dash of oil
  • 40g butter
  • 40g plain flour
  • 150mL vegetable stock
  • 150mL beef stock
  • 150mL thick double cream
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Melt 40g butter in a pan
  2. Whisk in 40g of plain flour and stir for two mins
  3. Add 150ml of veg stock and 150mL of beef stock and continue to stir
  4. Add 150ml of double cream, 2 tsp of soy sauce, and 1 tsp of Dijon mustard
  5. Bring it to simmer and allow sauce to thicken
